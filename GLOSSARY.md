# Glossary

## Purpose

Authoritative definitions of terms used in the Chocolate Manufacturing PMKSY Implementation Guide 2026. All documents must use these definitions consistently.

## Terms

### A

**Approved Project**
A project that has received formal sanction from MoFPI under PMKSY, including subsidy commitment letter.

### B

**Bloom (Fat Bloom / Sugar Bloom)**
Surface whitening on chocolate caused by fat or sugar crystallization; quality defect.

**Brownfield Project**
Expansion or modernization of an existing chocolate manufacturing facility.

### C

**CapEx (Capital Expenditure)**
Funds used to acquire, upgrade, or maintain physical assets (plant, machinery, buildings).

**Cold Chain**
Temperature-controlled supply chain; for chocolate: cool chain (15-18°C), not frozen.

**Conching**
Critical chocolate processing step: prolonged mixing, heating, and aeration to develop flavor and texture.

**Cocoa Butter Equivalent (CBE)**
Non-cocoa fat with similar properties to cocoa butter, permitted up to 5% under FSSAI.

**Cocoa Mass / Cocoa Liquor**
Ground cocoa nibs (liquid at processing temperature); primary chocolate ingredient.

**Commissioning**
Process of verifying all systems operate per design before commercial production.

### D

**Difficult Areas**
As defined by PMKSY: North Eastern States, Himalayan States, Islands, ITDP areas — eligible for higher subsidy.

### E

**Eligible Project Cost (EPC)**
Project cost components approved for subsidy calculation under PMKSY guidelines.

**Expansion Project**
Increase in installed capacity of existing facility by minimum 25% (PMKSY criterion).

### F

**Fat Bloom**
White/gray streaks on chocolate surface from unstable cocoa butter crystallization (Form VI).

**Food Processing Industry (FPI)**
Sector covered under PMKSY; chocolate manufacturing classified under FPI.

**Form V / Form VI**
Cocoa butter polymorphic forms; Form V (stable, glossy, snap) desired; Form VI causes bloom.

### G

**General Areas**
All areas not classified as Difficult Areas under PMKSY; standard subsidy rates apply.

**Greenfield Project**
New chocolate manufacturing facility on previously undeveloped land.

### H

**HACCP (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point)**
Systematic preventive approach to food safety; mandatory for FSSAI licensing.

### I

**Installed Capacity**
Maximum rated production output per annum (MT/year) as per equipment specifications.

**ITDP (Integrated Tribal Development Project)**
Tribal areas designated for higher PMKSY subsidy (50%).

### M

**Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF)**
Reliability metric for equipment; used in maintenance planning.

**MoFPI (Ministry of Food Processing Industries)**
Nodal ministry for PMKSY; scheme owner and sanctioning authority.

### N

**NABL (National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories)**
Accreditation body for testing labs; required for FSSAI compliance testing.

**New Unit**
Greenfield project or brownfield expansion meeting PMKSY "new unit" criteria.

### O

**OpEx (Operating Expenditure)**
Ongoing costs: raw materials, utilities, labor, maintenance, overheads.

### P

**PMKSY (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana)**
Central government scheme for food processing infrastructure; full name: प्रधानमंत्री किसान सम्पदा योजना.

**Polymorphism**
Ability of cocoa butter to crystallize in different forms (I-VI); critical for tempering.

**Processing Line**
Integrated equipment sequence: roasting → winnowing → grinding → refining → conching → tempering → moulding.

**Project Implementation Agency (PIA)**
Entity responsible for project execution; can be applicant or designated agency.

### Q

**Quality Control (QC)**
Operational techniques to fulfill quality requirements (testing, inspection).

**Quality Assurance (QA)**
Planned systematic activities to provide confidence in quality (systems, audits).

### R

**Refining**
Particle size reduction of chocolate mass to target fineness (typically 18-22 microns).

**Roasting**
Thermal treatment of cocoa beans (120-150°C) to develop flavor precursors.

### S

**Sanction Letter**
Official MoFPI document approving project and committing subsidy.

**Sugar Bloom**
Surface crystallization from moisture condensation dissolving and recrystallizing sugar.

### T

**Tempering**
Controlled crystallization of cocoa butter to Form V; critical for gloss, snap, shelf life.

**Tempering Index**
Measure of temper quality (typically 2.5-4.0 for well-tempered chocolate).

**Total Project Cost (TPC)**
Sum of all capital expenditures including contingencies, pre-operative expenses.

### U

**Utilization Ratio**
Actual production / Installed capacity; key performance indicator.

### V

**Value Addition**
Processing that increases product value; PMKSY eligibility criterion.

### W

**Winnowing**
Separation of cocoa nibs from shells after roasting.
