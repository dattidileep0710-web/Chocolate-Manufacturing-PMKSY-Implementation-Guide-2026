## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| PMKSY guideline changes | High | Monitor MoFPI notifications weekly; flag in OPEN_QUESTIONS.md |
| Missing BIS/FSSAI references | High | Engage standards experts early; maintain reference tracker |
| Financial model assumptions | Medium | Document all assumptions; sensitivity analysis in FINM-004 |
| Cross-reference debt | Medium | Automated link validation in CI; fix before phase gate |

## Resource Allocation

- 1 Technical Lead (architecture, technical specs)
- 1 Scheme Expert (PMKSY interpretation, eligibility)
- 1 Quality/Regulatory Lead (FSSAI, BIS, compliance)
- 1 Financial Analyst (cost models, subsidies)
- 1 Documentation Lead (style, indexing, control)
- 1 QA/Verification Lead (verification matrix, testing)
