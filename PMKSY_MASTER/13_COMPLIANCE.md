# Compliance

## Overview

This document defines the comprehensive statutory compliance framework for PMKSY-supported chocolate manufacturing projects in Andhra Pradesh. It covers all mandatory, conditional, and state-specific compliances required before, during, and after project implementation.

**OFFICIAL** — PMKSY projects must comply with all applicable central and state statutes. Non-compliance can result in subsidy recovery, project closure, or legal action.

> **SCOPE BOUNDARY**: This document addresses **statutory compliance requirements**. It does **not** address:
> - Eligibility criteria (see [PMKS-002: Project Eligibility](PMKSY_MASTER/02_PROJECT_ELIGIBILITY.md))
> - Cost eligibility (see [PMKS-006: Eligible Project Cost](PMKSY_MASTER/06_ELIGIBLE_PROJECT_COST.md))
> - Document requirements (see [PMKS-007: Required Documents](PMKSY_MASTER/07_REQUIRED_DOCUMENTS.md))
> - Application workflow (see [PMKS-008: Application Workflow](PMKSY_MASTER/08_APPLICATION_WORKFLOW.md))
> - Agency roles (see [PMKS-009: Implementing Agencies](PMKSY_MASTER/09_IMPLEMENTING_AGENCIES.md))
> - Appraisal criteria (see [PMKS-010: Project Appraisal](PMKSY_MASTER/10_PROJECT_APPRAISAL.md))
> - Grant release (see [PMKS-011: Grant Release](PMKSY_MASTER/11_GRANT_RELEASE.md))
> - Post-project monitoring (see [PMKS-012: Post-Project Monitoring](PMKSY_MASTER/12_POST_PROJECT_MONITORING.md))

## Environmental Compliances

### Detailed Environmental Requirements

**OFFICIAL** — Environmental compliance is governed by:
- Environment Protection Act, 1986
- Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974
- Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981
- EIA Notification, 2006 (as amended)
- AP-specific rules and notifications

### EIA Classification

**REQUIRES RE-VERIFICATION** — Environmental clearance category for ₹2 crore chocolate manufacturing:

| Scenario | Likely Category | Status |
|----------|----------------|--------|
| Chocolate manufacturing < 10,000 MT/year | General conditions or Category B | REQUIRES RE-VERIFICATION |
| Integrated cocoa processing + chocolate | Depends on combined capacity | REQUIRES RE-VERIFICATION |
| With boiler / DG set | General conditions likely | REQUIRES RE-VERIFICATION |

### Environmental Management Plan

**PROJECT ASSUMPTION** — Typical EMP for chocolate manufacturing:

| Environmental Aspect | Impact | Management | Status |
|----------------------|--------|------------|--------|
| Effluent | Wash water from cleaning, boiler blowdown | ETP / ZLD | REQUIRES RE-VERIFICATION |
| Emissions | Boiler flue gases | ESP / bag filter | REQUIRES RE-VERIFICATION |
| Solid waste | Cocoa shells, packaging waste | Composting / recycling / disposal | PROJECT ASSUMPTION |
| Noise | Machinery, generators | Acoustic enclosures | PROJECT ASSUMPTION |
| Energy | Power consumption | Energy-efficient equipment | PROJECT ASSUMPTION |
| Water | Process water, cooling | Recycling, rainwater harvesting | ENGINEERING RECOMMENDATION |

- Product-specific standards for chocolate and cocoa products
