# Common Rejection Reasons

## Overview

This document catalogs the most frequent reasons for **rejection, delays, or adverse decisions** throughout the PMKSY lifecycle for chocolate manufacturing projects in Andhra Pradesh. It is organized by project stage and provides prevention strategies and corrective actions for each issue.

**PROJECT ASSUMPTION** — The issues listed below are derived from common pitfalls observed in project documentation, appraisal practices, and implementation experience. They are **not** official PMKSY rejection criteria published by MoFPI. Official rejection grounds are defined in PMKSY scheme guidelines and implementing agency procedures.

> **SCOPE BOUNDARY**: This document addresses **rejection and delay reasons** across the PMKSY lifecycle. It does **not** address eligibility criteria, cost classification, document lists, or compliance requirements in detail — those are covered in their respective documents (PMKS-002 through PMKS-013).
